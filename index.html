1. Don’t Panic

Your safety will be in your hands, and the success of your plan will depend on your clear thinking.

2. Finding Water

Water is a resource for the human body to keep on living. We cannot live without water for more than three days. You can find the water source in the jungle area.

3. Finding Food

The resources for finding food are everywhere. Keep moving forward and look for something to eat as you go. Fishing will give you energy to keep moving.

4. Make A fire

A fire is important for any survival: it can make you warm, you can cook and keep off insect in the night time.

5. Walk In One General Direction

Focus on a reference point in the distance to go.

7. Beware of Predators

There are many predatory animals, so be careful. Do not make loud sounds, and walk as carefully as you can. Also, be alert to the sound you hear around you.

8. Beware of Mosquitos

Mosquitos in the jungle are spreading disease. The most dangerous is Malaria.
